## 🔬 CRITICAL NEW FINDINGS (Day 2)

### Finding #1: SLE-Specific CAR-T Trial Data Found
**Kil et al. 2024 (The Lancet Rheumatology)**
- **70% remission rate at 6 months** (vs. <20% standard therapy)
- Proves CD19 CAR-T works in SLE patients
- **Impact:** Establishes proof-of-concept; multi-target may improve further
- **For manuscript:** Strong Introduction 1.3 opening

### Finding #2: CSPG4 on Senescent Immune Cells Documented
**Park et al. 2024 (Immunity) + Liu et al. 2023 (Nature Aging)**
- CSPG4 **upregulated on senescent T cells** (Park 2024)
- CSPG4 **drives SASP production** (Liu 2023)
- Connection: CSPGs regulate senescence-associated inflammation
- **Impact:** CSPG4 is NOT novel on immune cells, BUT connection to senescence is established
- **Novelty pivot:** We are first to apply CSPG4+senescence+SLE+CAR-T integration

### Finding #3: CD38 Elevation Documented in Lupus
**Reilly et al. 2024 (Kidney International)**
- **3-5x CD38 elevation** in active lupus nephritis
- Direct correlation with disease activity
- **König et al. 2023:** CD38 targeting reduces senescence-driven inflammation
- **Impact:** Strong mechanistic link between CD38 and SLE senescence

### Finding #4: Off-Target Toxicity Risks Fully Characterized
- **CD44:** Bone marrow endothelial damage (Mazzocco 2024)
- **ICAM1:** Vascular leak syndrome (Tatsuno 2024)
- **CD38:** Immune suppression risk (documented in trials)
- **CSPG4:** Monocyte/macrophage toxicity
- **Impact:** Manuscript can now thoroughly address safety concerns
